Privacy Policy

Last Updated: December 2025

This Privacy Policy explains how YouTube Preview Popup ("we", "us", or "our") handles your information when you use our Chrome Extension.

1. Data Collection

We do not collect, store, or transmit any of your personal data.

The extension operates locally on your browser. It does not send your browsing history, YouTube watch history, or any other personal information to external servers.

2. Data Usage

The extension accesses specific websites (youtube.com) solely to:

  • Detect video links on the page.
  • Inject the "Preview" button.
  • Display the video player overlay.

Settings you configure (such as button position or preferred player size) are saved locally on your device using Chrome's storage.local API and are never shared.

3. Third-Party Services

The extension displays content from YouTube (Google). When you preview a video, you are interacting directly with YouTube's embedded player. As such, your interaction serves subject to YouTube's Terms of Service and Google's Privacy Policy.

4. Contact

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact us via the GitHub repository or the Chrome Web Store support page.
